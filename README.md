Australia's Tourism Story — 2025

This project is a data visualisation  created for FIT2179 Data Visualisation 2 at Monash University.

The visualisation explores Australia's tourism economy in 2025, focusing on domestic tourism, international visitors, visitor spending, state and territory comparisons, and tourism forecasts.

## Live Visualisation

https://alexislowys.github.io/2179_A2/

## Data Sources

- Tourism Research Australia — Domestic Tourism Statistics, December Quarter 2025
- Tourism Research Australia — International Visitor Survey Results, December Quarter 2025
- Tourism Research Australia — Visitor Economy Facts & Figures, May 2026 release
- Australian Bureau of Statistics — Overseas Arrivals and Departures, Australia
